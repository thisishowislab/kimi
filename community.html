Community Activated Relic Exchange
C.A.R.E.

Trade. Barter. Connect. For those who walk between worlds.

The Community Activated Relic Exchange is a space for Manifestorium visitors to trade goods, services, and time for our Artifacts. This is an experimental feature—use it wisely. Slab City operates beyond the edge of society where money isnt required for everything. We believe in circular micro-economies. If you have no currency but possess skills, materials, or time to devote to The Manifestorium, we may still trade.
